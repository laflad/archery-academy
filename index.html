Classes

A101 Basic Archery

A course that focuses on beginning archery safety, skills, and drills. Participants learn about archery form, range rules and etiquette.

Mondays and Wednesdays, 4-5pm

A102 Intermediate Archery

A course that focuses on intermediate archery safety, form, and introduces new equipment styles of shooting Recurve and Compound bows.

Tuesdays and Thursdays, 4-5pm

A103 Advanced Archery

A continued education towards tournament styles of shooting used by many archery tournament associations from around the world. Along with the tournament lesson, participants receive continued knowledge around their shooting form and advice on personal equipment selections.

Tuesdays and Thursdays, 5-6pm
